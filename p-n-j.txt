This is the best recipe for Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich
1. take 2 slices of bread out of the sliced bread plastic bag
2. Open jar of peanut butter
3. Get squeezable jelly jar
4. With a butter knife, scoop out peanut butter and spread onto bread
5. Then squeeze jelly on other slice.
6. Put both slices together
7. cut off crust if preferred
